The project

This project was undertaken as part of an academic assessment for the "Information and Technology Skills" course instructed by Professor Valentina Presutti. This particular course is included in the international Master's program titled "Language, Society, and Communication" at the University of Bologna. It was carried out by 3 Russian-speaking girls studying at the University of Bologna - Valeriia Ogorodnikova, Anastasiia Hulevych and Alena Nagovitcyna.

The project focuses on developing a data story on the "Melody" website, which centers around music and its historical significance. Our project comprises two stories, one dedicated to the Beatles and the other to Joy Division. Drawing inspiration from the field of data journalism, we conducted extensive data analysis to craft compelling narratives.

To accomplish this, we utilized the Polifonia corpora and the Corpus interrogation tool to explore the data in depth. We also delved into the vast knowledge graph of Wikidata, leveraging SPARQL queries to gather relevant information. By employing a data-driven approach, we unearthed intriguing facts and formulated hypotheses that we then tested and substantiated using the available data.

We have approached this task from two different angles: one involved delving into the data itself, uncovering intriguing facts, while the other entailed formulating hypotheses and questions and then employing data to test and substantiate them. Crafting these stories has been an engrossing and demanding journey on the Melody platform, which has taken us far beyond our usual realm of study. It has served as an opportunity to venture outside our comfort zone and broaden our knowledge.

Creating the stories on the Melody platform has been an engaging and challenging experience, expanding our knowledge beyond our usual areas of study. To ensure wider accessibility, we have published our website on GitHub. The website features two articles that encapsulate the essence of our stories and provide the academic foundation for our project. Additionally, it hosts a comprehensive project report that details the methodology employed throughout the creation process.

The report provides a thorough description of each step of the project, including the tools utilized, such as the Melody website and other relevant resources. It also discusses the challenges encountered during the project and the strategies we employed to overcome them.

Overall, the Melody project has been a journey of exploration and learning, allowing us to venture outside our comfort zone and expand our understanding of music history through the lens of data analysis and storytelling.

We are happy to present our Melody stories: The Beatles and Joy Division. Thank you for your attention!
